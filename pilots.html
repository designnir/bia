ABOUT: BIG ISLAND AIR PILOTS

Only the best pilots fly for Big Island Air.

Our pilots are the most experienced tour pilots in Hawaii. Not only does each one have extensive knowledge of the history, culture and geology of the Island, all are trained above and beyond the standards of the Federal Aviation Administration.

For training and testing, we use FlightSafety International, the leader in corporate aviation training. Fortune 500 companies and over 30 countries worldwide trust FlightSafety in the training of their pilots.
